About Me

Born in Casablanca, raised on rhythm.

With over a decade behind the decks, I blend the hypnotic rhythms of traditional Moroccan music with cutting-edge electronic production. From the medinas of Marrakech to the clubs of Ibiza, my sound carries the spirit of Morocco to dancefloors worldwide.

My sets are journeys — weaving Gnawa trance, deep house, and techno into unforgettable sonic experiences.
